Statistics and Baseball

These heat maps show approximately eight years of hitting data for MLB right-handed hitters. MLB Advanced Media collects and provides the PITCHf/x data. I used R for all analysis, dplyr for local MySQL database management, and created the heat maps in ggplot2. The map on the right depicts a GLM I fit to the spatial Bernoulli hitting data on the left.
